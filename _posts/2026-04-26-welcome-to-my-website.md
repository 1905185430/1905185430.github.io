Welcome to my technical blog! I'm excited to share my journey in robotics, artificial intelligence, and software development.

## About This Blog

This blog will serve as a platform for me to share:

1. **Technical insights** from my work on robot learning and VLA models
2. **Tutorials** on setting up and using various robotics tools
3. **Project updates** and progress reports
4. **Research notes** and findings

## My Background

I am a third-year undergraduate student at Huazhong University of Science and Technology, where I lead the Dian Team's robotics group. My research focuses on:

- **Robot Learning**: Imitation learning and reinforcement learning for robotic manipulation
- **Vision-Language-Action Models**: Fine-tuning and deploying VLA models for robot control
- **Computer Vision**: 3D perception and visual servoing

## Current Projects

### SO-101 Robot Arm System
I've been developing a comprehensive system for SO-101 dual-arm robot control. This includes:

- Teleoperation with real-time feedback
- Automated calibration procedures
- Data collection pipelines
- Policy training and deployment

### LeRobot Framework
I contribute to HuggingFace's LeRobot framework, which provides standardized tools for robot learning. My contributions include:

- Dataset management utilities
- Training pipeline improvements
- Deployment tools

### VLA Model Fine-tuning
I'm working on tools for fine-tuning Vision-Language-Action models like SmolVLA for specific robotic tasks. This involves:

- Data preparation scripts
- Training infrastructure
- Evaluation frameworks
